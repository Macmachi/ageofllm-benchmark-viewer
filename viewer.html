Goal of the game

A 1v1 duel between two AIs. Each turn both players take up to 3 actions. Win by destroying the enemy base before the turn limit (default 80) — by nuclear bomb or military conquest (tanks reduce the base to 0 HP) — or by an accepted ultimatum. A peace or the turn limit ends in a draw. Fog of war hides the enemy half: you must scout.

Triangle: Fighter > Tank/Drone/Fighter · Tank > SAM/Tank/buildings · SAM > air units. Only the Tank can hit buildings (2 HP/hit). Attacker survives (mirror rule). Line of sight: a ground attack (tank/sam) is blocked by a mountain or building between shooter and target — flank around cover. The fighter (air) ignores obstacles.

Construction: operational on the next turn. Under construction = no effect + destroyed in 1 hit. You can only build on a visible, free cell. One building per cell; cells adjacent to a base are reserved for unit spawning. Mines can go on any free matching deposit you see — incl. the enemy's side (no instant capture: destroy the enemy mine or wait for its deposit to run dry, then claim it); the silo stays own-territory only. Deposits deplete: a drained deposit removes its mine and respawns elsewhere on the same side (central on column 6).

Nuclear bomb

Cost 25☢ (drops after T40, floor 13). Requires an operational silo + uranium ≥ cost + enemy base discovered. Launches are secret & simultaneous: resolved at end of turn — a double launch = mutual destruction.

Map

The central barrier (column 6), the in-territory mountains and all deposit positions are seed-driven — they differ every match. Mountains block ground movement, construction and line of sight for ground attacks (air flies over).
